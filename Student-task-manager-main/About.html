Features & Capabilities

Stay organized, focused, and on top of your academic responsibilities effortlessly. Here is a look at the core features of the Student Task Manager:

  • Clean & Interactive UI: Enjoy a simple, user-friendly interface that makes managing tasks quick and stress-free.
  • Track Assignments: Never miss a deadline by logging all your homework, essays, and major projects.
  • Prioritize Work: Sort your to-do lists by urgency or subject to ensure you tackle the most important goals first.
  • Set Reminders: Keep track of upcoming exams and assignment submission dates with easy reminders.
  • Smart Alerts: Get notified about upcoming deadlines and pending tasks.
  • Progress Dashboard: View charts or summaries of completed and pending tasks.
  • Monitor Progress: Visualize your work completion to see how close you are to achieving your study objectives.
  • Study Planning: Break down large syllabus topics or exam prep into manageable daily milestones.
  • Dark Mode Support: Switch between light and dark themes for comfortable viewing anytime.
