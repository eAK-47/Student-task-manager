Stay organized, focused, and on top of your academic responsibilities effortlessly. Here is a look at the core features of the platform.

Enjoy a simple, user-friendly interface that makes managing tasks quick and stress-free.

📋

Track Assignments

Never miss a deadline by logging all your homework, essays, and major projects.

⚡

Prioritize Work

Sort your to-do lists by urgency or subject to ensure you tackle the most important goals first.

Smart Alerts

Get notified about upcoming deadlines and pending tasks so nothing slips through the cracks.

Progress Dashboard

Monitor Progress

Visualize your work completion to see how close you are to achieving your study objectives.

Study Planning

Break down large syllabus topics or exam prep into manageable daily milestones.

Dark Mode Support

Switch between light and dark themes for comfortable viewing anytime, anywhere.
